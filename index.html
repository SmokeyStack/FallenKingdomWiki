About Fallen Kingdom

Fallen Kingdom is a an add-on developed by SmokeyStack. Gameplay involves trying to survive the aftermath of The 60 Seconds. What is The 60 Seconds? No one knows. All they know is that it took 60 Seconds for the world to realize their mistake. Those who died were fortunate for those who survived had to face the aftermath and the Fallen Kingdom.
